Runs on your network

Laptime Insights is self-hosted. You download it and run it on a PC on the same network as your Assetto Corsa Competizione server — no cloud account, and your telemetry never leaves your network.

1

Download & run

Grab the latest release and start it on any PC on your home network — the same network your ACC server runs on.

2

It listens to ACC

It connects to your ACC server's telemetry feed and records every session and lap into a local database on your machine.

3

Open the dashboard

Open the dashboard in your browser, from your PC or any device on the same network, and dig into your performance.

Everything in one dashboard

Four screens that take you from "how much have I driven" down to "which corner cost me the lap".

Overview

Sessions, laps, and driving time at a glance, plus activity charts, a tracks-practiced bubble chart, and your all-time best lap per track.

Sessions

Every session, filterable by track, car, and simulator. Filters live in the URL so any view is bookmarkable and shareable.

Laps

A searchable, paginated list of every lap. Filter to valid laps or personal bests and jump straight to the parent session.

Compare

Overlay two laps' speed traces, a 2-D track map, a speed-delta chart, and gear mismatches — linked crosshairs show exactly where time is gained or lost.

Streaks

A consecutive-days streak keeps you honest about your practice cadence — and tells you the moment it breaks.

Time-range analytics

One global filter (1M / 3M / 6M / 1Y / All) re-scopes every stat, chart, and list — and your choice survives a reload.
